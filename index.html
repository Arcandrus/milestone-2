Welcome to my Magic: the Gathering Memory Game!

The object of the game is to find all the pairs in the fastest time you can. The faster you go, the higher your score will be.

To start the game, click on the difficulty level you wish to attempt using the buttons above.

You can always restart the game at any time by clicking the same or another difficulty button.

When you've won the game by finding all the matches, you will be invited to save your score, and enter it on your High Scores display.

You can see this display at any time by clicking the "Show High Scores" button.

Using colorblind mode will change some features to be easier to see, and will add text to the cards, a great way to learn the guild names!

I hope you enjoy your experience with my game and good luck!
